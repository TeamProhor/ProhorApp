Welcome to the Prohor Quickstart guide. Follow these simple steps to kickstart your project ideation and access your custom Prohor Cloud workbench.

## Step 1: Initialize Your Partnership

Get in touch with our co-founding team to pitch your concept:

```bash
curl -fsSL https://api.prohor.com/pitch.sh | bash -s -- --idea "your-next-unicorn"
```

Alternatively, book an ideation call through our Web Dashboard.

## Step 2: Access Your Prohor Platform

Login to the Prohor Console to trace your build cycles, API deployments, and marketing calendars:

```bash
prohor auth login
```

## Step 3: Monitor Your MVP Build

Track the status of your app development and cloud server setups:

```bash
prohor status --project my-startup-mvp
```

You can view active builds, security scan reports, and database links directly on the dashboard tab!
